SHARON OH

My name is Sharon Oh. I am a current honors student at the New Jersey Institute of Technology pursuing a Bachelors of Science in Human-Computer Interaction. I am interested in utilizing creative and abstract ways of thinking in my future career. I plan to go into UX design or Product design, or any role that entails imagination in a problem-solving setting.

CREATIVE EXPRESSION

I have grown up always loving to create. The power of creation and using my imagination to build something out of nothing. I love doing hands-on things where I can create with different mediums. I love baking and cooking, crocheting, drawing, painting, sculpting, fashion, and even non-traditional mediums of creative expression like painting nails, doing hair, etcetera.
