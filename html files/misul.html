ART GROWING UP

I have been interested in art ever since I was a child. I took separate art lessons in elementary school to learn foundational skills. Throughout my four years of high school, I took multiple art classes that allowed me to experiment with different types of mediums such as clay, ink, oil paints, etcetera. In my senior year of high school, I took AP Studio Art that enabled me to develop a portfolio of multiple pieces and cater it to a central theme.

LEARNED LESSONS

Taking many art classes made me realize that art is a very difficult and subjective field. People stereotype against artistic fields as they think it is easy and uncomplicated, but it requires patience, perseverance, and a growth mentality. Receiving constant criticism on certain pieces made me occasionally doubt myself, but it also allowed me to improve my craft and develop resilience and motivation to do better.
